Axe Throw 1.0.3
- Wow, it's been a long time
  - Add new DING sound (experience orb) to let the user know when their item is ready to throw
  - Add new gamerule `axe_throw:notify_when_throw_ready` that controls whether the notify sound plays
  - Thrown weapons now charge faster with faster attack speeds
  - Thrown weapons now have slightly higher or lower gravity based on attack speed
  - Thrown weapons now deal damage based on attack damage
